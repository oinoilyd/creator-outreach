## Notes + open questions

- **Discount design (item 4):** what's actually best for conversion?
  - $20/mo locked forever (lifetime founder pricing) — strongest signal of value, hardest to walk back later
  - 2 months free at $50 → then full $50/mo — easier to upsell, less margin compression
  - 50% off first 6 months — middle ground
  - **Need to decide before launch.**

- **Demo video vs founder video** — both get linked in the warm intro:
  - **Founder video (Ryan):** 2-4 min, emotional, builds trust, the WHY
  - **Demo video (Dylan):** 3-5 min, practical, builds belief, the WHAT
  - Order in intro email: founder video first (hook) → "if you want to see the product itself, here's a 5-min demo"

- **Testimonial collection** (item 7) ideas:
  - Slack/email after their first successful outreach campaign — "got a sec for a 1-line quote?"
  - In-app banner asking after 14 days of use
  - Founder DM if they look engaged in the app

- **Insurance and Supabase Pro upgrade** — defer until first paying customer.

- **The 100-lead conversion target:**
  - 5% = 5 customers = $250 MRR = $3,000 ARR (good baseline)
  - 10% = 10 customers = $500 MRR = $6,000 ARR (strong launch)
  - 15%+ = strong founder-led sales signal, hire-decision territory
