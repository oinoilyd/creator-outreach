# Founders Agreement — Creator Outreach

**Date:** ________________, 2026
**Parties:**
- **Dylan Meehan** (residing at 1749 N Wells St, Chicago, IL 60614) — "Dylan"
- **Ryan Gaynor** (residing at ____________________________________) — "Ryan"

**Entity:** Gaynor Media LLC, an Illinois limited liability company ("Company")
**Product:** Creator Outreach (a SaaS for creator discovery and outreach automation), operated by the Company under the assumed business name "Creator Outreach" once filed with the Illinois Secretary of State.

This agreement governs the relationship between Dylan and Ryan as co-founders of the Creator Outreach product. Where this agreement is silent or in conflict, the Company's operating agreement and Illinois LLC law govern.

---

## 1. Roles & Day-to-Day Operations

- **Dylan:** Operating co-founder and product architect. Owns product, engineering, customer-facing operations, marketing, and customer support. Day-to-day responsible for the build and direct outreach to prospects and customers.

- **Ryan:** Strategic co-founder, **co-originator of the Creator Outreach concept**, and ongoing industry advisor. Ryan brought the original business concept and market opportunity to Dylan, provided the domain expertise that informed the product's positioning and feature set, and continues to advise on industry strategy. Owns the LLC entity (Gaynor Media LLC), provides industry network access, makes warm introductions to prospects, and supports legal/operational matters. Ryan does NOT solicit, contact, or sell to clients of his current employer.

Both founders mutually acknowledge that Creator Outreach would not exist without the combination of Ryan's industry knowledge and concept-origination, and Dylan's product and engineering execution. Neither role is greater than the other; both are essential.

Both founders share oversight of strategic direction, finances, and major decisions per Section 3.

## 2. Revenue Split

Net revenue (gross revenue less direct costs: payment processing, infrastructure, vendor fees, taxes) is split as follows:

- **Dylan: 55%**
- **Ryan: 45%**

Distributions occur quarterly from any positive net revenue, after retaining reasonable operating reserves agreed by both founders. Either founder may waive a distribution to reinvest in the Company.

## 3. Decision-Making

All material decisions require unanimous (50/50) consent of both founders. "Material decisions" include:

- New products or pivots away from the Creator Outreach product
- Hiring or terminating employees / contractors with annual cost > $5,000
- Taking on debt or external financing
- Selling, merging, dissolving the Company
- Equity issuance or revenue-share changes
- Changing pricing tiers or sunsetting the product

Day-to-day product and operating decisions within Dylan's role do NOT require unanimous consent. Day-to-day industry-network and LLC-entity decisions within Ryan's role do NOT require unanimous consent.

## 4. Sunset / Cost-Recovery Provision

If the founders mutually agree to sunset the Creator Outreach product BEFORE cumulative net revenue covers cumulative direct costs to date:

- The remaining unrecovered direct costs (covered by Ryan/the LLC up to that point) shall be split **50/50 between Dylan and Ryan**, payable to the LLC within 90 days of sunset.
- "Direct costs" for this purpose include: Vercel Pro, Supabase Pro, Stripe processing fees, vendor SaaS (Unipile, Anthropic API, etc.), domain registration, DBA filing fees, insurance premiums, and any other Company-paid infrastructure or operational expense.
- Costs that have ALREADY been covered by Company revenue (or by Dylan personally) are not subject to this split.

## 5. Intellectual Property

All IP created in connection with Creator Outreach — including code, designs, copy, customer lists, brand assets, and goodwill — belongs to the LLC. Both founders assign all rights, title, and interest in such IP to the LLC.

Code that existed before this agreement and was contributed by Dylan to the product is irrevocably assigned to the LLC.

Neither founder may use Company IP for personal projects or outside ventures without written consent from the other.

## 6. Confidentiality

Each founder will keep Company business information confidential and not disclose it to third parties without consent, except as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., advisors, accountants, prospective customers under standard NDAs).

Ryan will NOT share confidential information from his current employer with the Company. The Company will NOT solicit, contact, or sell to clients of Ryan's current employer.

## 7. Non-Compete Release Between Founders

Neither founder is restricted from working on or owning other businesses outside the Creator Outreach product, provided those businesses do NOT directly compete with Creator Outreach (defined as: a SaaS product offering creator discovery + outreach automation in the same target segments).

Ryan may continue his current employment without conflict, subject to his obligation under Section 6 to not bring confidential employer information into the Company.

## 8. Term, Departure, and Buyout

This agreement remains in effect until:

- Mutual written agreement to terminate
- Sale of the Company (proceeds split per Section 2 after debts and direct costs)
- Voluntary departure of a founder (departing founder forfeits future revenue share but retains their share of any net revenue earned up to departure date; equity buyout terms TBD by mutual agreement)
- Involuntary departure for cause (gross misconduct, breach of this agreement, conviction of a felony involving moral turpitude — buyout at fair market value as determined by both founders or a neutral third party)

## 9. Disputes & Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of Illinois. Any dispute will first be resolved through good-faith discussion between the founders. If unresolved within 30 days, the founders will mediate before pursuing arbitration in Cook County, Illinois.

## 10. Entire Agreement

This document represents the entire agreement between Dylan and Ryan regarding Creator Outreach. It supersedes any prior verbal or written agreements. Amendments require written signature from both founders.
